\subsubsection{Stirling numbers of the second kind}
Partitions of $n$ distinct elements into exactly $k$ groups. 

\[ S(n, k) = S(n - 1, k - 1) + kS(n - 1, k), S(n, 1) = S(n, n) = 1 \]
\[ S(n, k) = \frac{1}{k!}\sum_{i=0}^{k}(-1)^{k-i}{k \choose i}i^n \]
